## 为什么要写下来？

> [!quote]
> 写作是思考的延伸。你不写下它，它就从未真正存在过。

每天花 10 分钟记录自己的想法，日积月累，你会发现：

1. 思维更加清晰
2. 知识内化更快
3. 创造力提升

> [!tip] 建议
> 不要追求完美，先写下来再说。好的想法往往来自于持续的输出，而非等待灵感的降临。

## Why Write Things Down?

> [!quote]
> Writing is the extension of thought. If you don't write it down, it never truly existed.

Spend 10 minutes daily recording your thoughts, and over time you'll find:

1. Clearer thinking
2. Faster knowledge internalization
3. Enhanced creativity

> [!tip] Suggestion
> Don't pursue perfection — just write first. Good ideas often come from consistent output, not waiting for inspiration.
